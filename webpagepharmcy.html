Department of Pharmacy

It gives me immense pleasure to lead the Department of Pharmacy. Our institute is one of the best institutions among Pharmacy institutes running Diploma in Pharmacy Course in Maharashtra, unique like a prism reflecting the manifold shades of learning and co-curricular activities. Department of Pharmacy is working very hard towards the goal of providing innovative and quality education with high standard to achieve academic excellence in field of Community Pharmacy and to provide the highly skilled Pharmacist in the service of Nation. The primary motive of our department is to provide quality education to our students. The process of learning is extremely important in life. What you learn, how you learn and where you learn play a crucial role in developing one’s intellectual capability, besides career. The excellent infrastructure, teaching faculty of the best kind ensuring quality education such as interaction among students, parents, and staff, along with a Training and Placement Cell. Many our brilliant students have been recruited in Government Services. Thus, we are confident that our Pharmacists will emerge as assets not only to this institution and to the organization they belong, but also to the country at large.
