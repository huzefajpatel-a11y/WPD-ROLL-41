Department of Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering department was established in the year 1961. Department is currently celebrating diamond jubilee year. Department is known for its tradition of imparting technical knowledge to students. Teaching staff available in department is highly experienced and with profound knowledge. Alumni of the department are working in industry and academia at key positions and they have brightened the name of department and the institute as well. Well-equipped laboratory and classrooms are the strengths of the department. Department also organizes numerous curricular and extra-curricular activities for the overall development of the students.

Vision

“To Develop Competent Electrical Technicians for Serving Society.”

Mission

To provide an effective learning environment.
To inculcate safe operating practices.
To provide exposure to students regarding tools used in the area of Electrical engineering.
To establish Industry Institute Interaction.
